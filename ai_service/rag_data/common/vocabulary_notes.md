# IELTS Lexical Resource – Band Descriptors

## Band 9 – Vocabulary
- Uses a wide range of vocabulary with full flexibility and precision.
- Sophisticated word choice with:
  - Accurate collocations
  - Idiomatic expressions (used naturally)
- No noticeable errors in word formation or usage.
- Vocabulary fully supports nuanced meaning.

## Band 8 – Vocabulary
- Wide vocabulary used fluently and flexibly.
- Occasional inaccuracies in word choice, but meaning is clear.
- Good use of:
  - Collocations
  - Topic-specific vocabulary
- Rare spelling or word formation errors.

## Band 7 – Vocabulary
- Sufficient range of vocabulary to discuss topics effectively.
- Some less common lexical items used appropriately.
- Occasional errors in word choice, collocation, or word form.
- Vocabulary generally supports meaning, though precision may vary.

## Band 6 – Vocabulary
- Adequate vocabulary for familiar topics.
- Limited ability to use less common lexical items.
- Noticeable repetition of words.
- Errors in word choice or form may reduce clarity at times.

## Band 5 – Vocabulary
- Limited vocabulary range.
- Frequent repetition of basic words.
- Errors in word choice often affect meaning.
- Inability to paraphrase effectively.

## Band 4 and below – Vocabulary
- Very basic vocabulary only.
- Frequent misuse of words.
- Meaning is often unclear due to lexical errors.

## Common issues:
- Overusing memorized phrases
- Informal words in essays
- Incorrect collocations
- Spelling affecting clarity
